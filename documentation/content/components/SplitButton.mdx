## Accessibility

SplitButton is exposed to assistive technology as a group containing a [Button](/components/Button) and a [Dropdown](/components/Dropdown) Button.
When focus is on the Button, <kbd>Alt</kbd>+<kbd>ArrowDown</kbd> will expand the Dropdown [Menu](/components/Menu), while <kbd>Space</kbd> or <kbd>Enter</kbd> will trigger a click event on the Button.
When focus is on the Dropdown Button, <kbd>ArrowDown</kbd>, <kbd>Space</kbd> or <kbd>Enter</kbd> will expand the Dropdown Menu.
The <kbd>Escape</kbd> key will close the Dropdown Menu.

### Labelling

The `label` prop provides the accessible name for the Button, Dropdown Button and containing group.

```jsx
<SplitButton
  label="Action"
  variant="primary">
  <MenuItem icon={<Twitter />} value="twitter">
```
