## Overview

A **Avatar** is used to signify. Banners cannot be interacted with. Banners in Spectrum have three variations for different uses as well as the ability to place it overlaid in the top-right corner of a container.

## Internationalization

To internationalize an avatar, a localized string should be passed to the alt prop of the avatar.

## Accessibility

Avatars are accessible by default. The correct aria roles will automatically be applied.
