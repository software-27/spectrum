# Community

react-spectrum is not built by a centralized team but by an active community all around Adobe, across many products and groups.

If you have questions about using react-spectrum, it is likely that someone in the community can help. Join us in #react-spectrum on Slack! It's also possible that your question has already been answered in our [FAQ](/guides/faq).
